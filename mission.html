Apollo 11

About Mission

On July 16, 1969, three brave astronauts launched into space on the historic Apollo 11 mission, which would go down as one of humanity's greatest achievements. As the spacecraft approached the lunar surface, the entire world watched in awe as Neil Armstrong spoke the immortal words, "One small step for man, one giant leap for mankind." The Apollo 11 mission captured the world's imagination by demonstrating human ingenuity and the never-ending pursuit of knowledge; this website invites you to relive the wonder of that historic moment and learn the incredible story behind humanity's first steps on the moon.

Mission Impact

  • First humans to land and walk on the lunar surface
  • Collected 47.5 lbs of lunar material for study back on Earth
  • Inspired millions around the world and ignited renewed passion for space travel
